About Autism Ambassadors

We are part of a local Autism charity and to source our news. Our project in the community is called the Autism Ambassadors. The autism Ambassadors were set up in 2010 in response to local instance in following a lack of autism awareness. We support people with Autism, delivering presentations on what it is like to have autism and how other people can supported those with Autism.

We provide presentions to all sectors in the borough upon request to companies and chairties. If you are interested to know more about autism and would like us to present information Contact Us.
